A website designed and developed by visionary teenagers endeavouring to bring the limelight to local artisians via a the typical modern way, a website. This website explores a vast array of information from popular arts of local artisians to ways to purchase them. This helps local artisians to promote their business as well as customers to purchase authentic work. हस्तकला Bazaar is a non-profit entity/ organization to urge people to purchase and encourage local artisians' businesses as well as their arts. Please purchase any handicraft from the site by signing in to the website and thus, helping these Artisians to coninue their work and additionally, bring elegance to your home. Have a good purchase!
